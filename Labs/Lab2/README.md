# Lab 2 — Applying AI in Healthcare Contexts

**Course:** ITAI 4376 — AI Applications
**Instructor:** Professor Patricia McManus
**Student:** Muhammad Umer
**Institution:** Houston City College | Spring 2026

## Overview

This lab explored how artificial intelligence is being applied in healthcare settings, with a focus on clinical decision support, predictive analytics, and the use of electronic health record data.

## What I Learned

I gained an understanding of how healthcare data differs from general-purpose datasets, particularly in terms of privacy constraints and the consequences of model errors in clinical contexts.

## Challenges Faced

Understanding the nature of healthcare data, which is often incomplete and subject to strict regulatory requirements such as HIPAA, was the primary challenge.

## Tools Used

Python, Jupyter Notebook, pandas, scikit-learn, Synthea synthetic EHR data

## Key Takeaway

Applying AI in healthcare requires technical skill combined with a deep respect for patient privacy and the ethical responsibilities that come with building systems that influence medical decisions.
